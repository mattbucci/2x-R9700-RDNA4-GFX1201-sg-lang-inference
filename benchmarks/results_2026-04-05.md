## FP8 on SGLang Status
**BLOCKED**: Triton on gfx1201 generates invalid ISA causing GPU hangs.
Torch-native FP8 replacements implemented (matmul, MoE, linear dispatch)
but remaining non-FP8 Triton kernels (RMSNorm, activation) also crash
during fresh compilation. Use vLLM Docker for FP8 models.

## Key Findings
1. **MoE models scale excellently**: Coder-30B FP8 achieves 1185 tok/s at 32 concurrent (vLLM)
2. **AWQ is the best SGLang path**: Devstral-24B achieves 858 tok/s at 32 concurrent
3. **Dense 27B models are bandwidth-limited**: Qwen3.5-27B plateaus at 129 tok/s regardless of concurrency
4. **Triton on gfx1201 is fundamentally broken for FP8**: All FP8 Triton kernels crash, and fresh compilation of any Triton kernel can trigger GPU hangs
5. **vLLM Docker is the reliable FP8 path**: Uses torch.compile/inductor, avoiding Triton entirely
